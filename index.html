🌿 VERDE CITY

An eco-futuristic city with four playable sports arenas. Explore on foot, teleport between arenas, and play Soccer, Basketball, Baseball, and Football. Works on desktop (WASD + mouse), mobile (touch joysticks), and Meta Quest 3 (WebXR — tap "Enter VR" once loaded).

Choose a character, then use Q (or ABILITY) for their special move. Desktop: Soccer SPACE/F · Basketball X/C · Baseball Z · Football V.
Mobile: left stick to move, drag right side to look, tap the green button to act.
